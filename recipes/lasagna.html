Lasagna Recipe

Making lasagna at home doesn't have to be tedious here is a quick and easy recipe that you can make at home whether you have prior experience in cooking or not!

The ingredients:

  • Beef
  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Cheeses: Cottage cheese, Mozzarella, and Parmesan
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings: Salt, Black Pepper, Dried Parsley
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Water
