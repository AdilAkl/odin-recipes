Lasagna Recipe

Making lasagna at home doesn't have to be tedious here is a quick and easy recipe that you can make at home whether you have prior experience in cooking or not!

The ingredients

  • Beef
  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Cheeses: Cottage cheese, Mozzarella, and Parmesan
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings: Salt, Black Pepper, Dried Parsley
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Water

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer.
  2. Combine the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, half of the Parmesan, and seasonings.
  3. Assemble the lasagna according to the detailed recipe.
  4. Bake, covered, for 45 minutes.
  5. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes.
